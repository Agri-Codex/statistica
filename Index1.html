🌿 What is Statistica?

Statistica is a free, fully offline, web-based statistical software designed for plant breeders, agronomists, geneticists, and agricultural researchers. It runs entirely in your browser — no installation, no server, no data leaves your computer.

Developed under the Agri-Codex initiative at the University of Agriculture, Faisalabad (UAF), it covers the full statistical workflow from exploratory data analysis to advanced inferential and non-parametric methods used in peer-reviewed PBG research.

🌾 Who Is It For?
  • Plant Breeders analyzing genotype × environment interactions
  • Geneticists studying trait heritability and variance components
  • Agronomists comparing treatment means across field trials
  • MSc / PhD students performing thesis statistical analysis
  • Faculty members teaching applied statistics in agriculture
  • Researchers preparing publications in agricultural journals

📄 How to Use Statistica
1
Import or Enter Data
Click ⇧ Import Data to upload a CSV/TXT file, or paste data directly. Or manually type values in any input field.
2
Select Your Test
Navigate the left sidebar to choose the appropriate statistical test based on your research question and data type.
3
Configure & Run
Set parameters (alpha level, tails, group labels), then click the green ▶ Analyze / Run Test button.
4
Interpret Results
Results include test statistics, p-values, effect sizes, CIs, and a plain-language decision with significance verdict.
🔒 100% Private & Offline

All calculations happen inside your browser using JavaScript. No data is uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server. Your research data remains completely confidential — safe for unpublished trial data.

⚠ Scope & Limitations

Currently supports univariate and bivariate analyses. Multi-factor ANOVA (factorial, split-plot, RCBD) and GxE analysis are planned for the next version. For complex mixed-model analyses, R or SAS remains recommended.
